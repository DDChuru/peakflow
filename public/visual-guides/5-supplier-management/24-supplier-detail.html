Screen Guide

Click on numbered badges or hover over UI elements to learn more about each feature.

1

Supplier Name and Contact

Comprehensive supplier header with name, status, email, phone, address, and tax ID. Provides complete contact overview for quick reference and communication.

Technical Details
  • Data Source: selectedSupplier from View Dialog
  • Fields: name, email, phone, address, taxId, status
  • Component: Supplier header section in dialog
  • Type: Creditor object
2

Payment Terms and Discount Info

Displays payment terms (e.g., Net 30) and any early payment discount options. Critical for cash flow planning and taking advantage of cost-saving opportunities.

Technical Details
  • Field: Creditor.paymentTerms (days)
  • Display: "Net {days}" format
  • Optional: earlyPaymentDiscount if available
  • Used For: Due date calculation on bills
3

Current AP Balance

Total outstanding accounts payable for this supplier. Updated in real-time as bills are recorded or payments processed. Key metric for supplier relationship management.

Technical Details
  • Field: Creditor.currentBalance
  • Calculation: Sum of unpaid bills minus unapplied credits
  • Updates: Real-time via Firestore listeners
  • Color: Red when balance > 0, gray when 0
4

Payment History

Historical record of all payments made to this supplier. Shows payment dates, amounts, and on-time status. Used to calculate payment score and reliability metrics.

Technical Details
  • Data Source: Payment transactions filtered by supplier
  • Service: PaymentService.getPaymentsByCreditor()
  • Displays: Payment date, amount, method, on-time status
  • Future: Link to payment receipts and bank records
5

AP Aging Breakdown

Visual breakdown of payables by age: Current, 1-30 days, 31-60 days, 61-90 days, and 90+ days overdue. Helps identify payment priorities and potential cash flow issues.

Technical Details
  • Calculation: Groups bills by days past due date
  • Service: APAgingService.getAgingByCreditor()
  • Buckets: Current, 1-30, 31-60, 61-90, 90+
  • Visual: Color-coded boxes (green for current, escalating warnings)
6

Transaction History Tabs

Tabbed interface for viewing different transaction types: Bills (vendor bills), Purchase Orders (POs sent), Payments (payments made), and Performance (analytics). Organizes supplier data for easy access.

Technical Details
  • Component: Tab navigation with state management
  • Tabs: Bills, POs, Payments, Performance
  • State: activeTab controls visible content
  • Data: Each tab queries different Firestore collections
7

Bill List for Supplier

Complete list of all bills from this supplier showing bill number, due date, amount, status (paid/unpaid), and line item details. Click to view full bill or process payment.

Technical Details
  • Data Source: Bills filtered by supplierId
  • Service: BillService.getBillsByCreditor()
  • Type: VendorBill[] from mock-data.json
  • Display: Sorted by date descending, color-coded by status
8

Performance Metrics

Supplier performance analytics including on-time delivery rate, service quality score, and cost competitiveness. Uses historical data to rate supplier performance and inform procurement decisions.

Technical Details
  • Field: Creditor.performanceMetrics (optional)
  • Metrics: On-time delivery, quality, cost, responsiveness
  • Calculation: Based on PO receipts, bill accuracy, pricing
  • Future: Integration with procurement and inventory systems
9

Preferred Supplier Indicator

Badge indicating this supplier has "Preferred" status based on excellent performance, competitive pricing, and reliability. Preferred suppliers get priority in procurement processes.

Technical Details
  • Field: Creditor.preferredSupplier (boolean)
  • Criteria: Performance > 90%, payment history, pricing
  • Benefits: Priority in PO creation, auto-selection in forms
  • Display: Green shield icon with "Preferred" text
10

Quick Actions

Action buttons for common supplier operations: New PO (create purchase order), Record Bill (enter vendor bill), Make Payment (process AP payment). Streamlines supplier transaction workflows.

Technical Details
  • Actions: New PO, Record Bill, Make Payment
  • Navigation: Routes to respective forms with supplier pre-filled
  • Services: POService, BillService, PaymentService
  • Context: Supplier ID passed to maintain data integrity
11

Spend Analysis

YTD (year-to-date) and MTD (month-to-date) spend analysis with year-over-year comparison. Helps track spending trends, identify cost-saving opportunities, and support budgeting decisions.

Technical Details
  • Calculation: Sum of all paid bills by date range
  • Service: SpendAnalyticsService.getSpendByCreditor()
  • Metrics: YTD, MTD, YoY growth percentage
  • Future: Budget comparison, forecasting, category breakdown
12

Notes and Documents Section

Internal notes for supplier context and document repository for contracts, tax forms, agreements, and correspondence. Centralizes supplier-related information for easy team access.

Technical Details
  • Notes Field: Creditor.notes (text)
  • Documents: Firebase Storage references with metadata
  • Service: DocumentService.getDocumentsByCreditor()
  • Features: Upload, download, versioning, access control
