Screen Guide

Bank statements list with filtering and status tracking.

Bank Account Filter

Filter statements by bank account to view specific account history.

2

Period Filter

Filter by month or date range to find specific statement periods.

3

Statements Table

Shows upload date, period, account, balances, and processing status for each statement.

Reconciliation Status Indicator

Shows statement processing status: Completed (green), Processing (yellow), Pending (gray), Failed (red).

5

Actions (View, Reconcile, Export)
