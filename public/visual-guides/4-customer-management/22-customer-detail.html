Screen Guide

Click on numbered badges or hover over UI elements to learn more about each feature.

1

Customer Name and Contact Info

Displays complete customer master data including legal name, email, phone, physical address, and VAT/Tax ID. This forms the foundation of the customer profile.

Technical Details
  • Data Source: selectedCustomer state
  • Fields: name, email, phone, address, taxId
  • Editable: Click "Edit Customer" button to modify
  • Validation: Email format, required fields enforced
2

Contact Information Display

Primary contact details for communication. Email is used for invoice delivery, phone for follow-ups. Supports multiple contacts via Financial Contacts Manager (shown below).

Technical Details
  • Primary Contact: customer.email, customer.phone
  • Additional Contacts: Managed via FinancialContactsManager
  • Integration: Links to email client (mailto:) on click
3

Credit Limit Section

Maximum credit extended to this customer. Used to calculate available credit and trigger credit hold warnings when exceeded.

Technical Details
  • Field: customer.creditLimit
  • Currency: ZAR with 2 decimal formatting
  • Business Logic: Enforced in invoice creation workflow
  • Editable: Only by financial admin or higher
4

Current Balance (AR)

Total outstanding accounts receivable for this customer. Sum of all unpaid and partially paid invoices. Updates in real-time as invoices are created and payments recorded.

Technical Details
  • Field: customer.currentBalance
  • Calculation: Sum of invoice.amountDue for customer
  • Updates: On invoice creation, payment recording
  • GL Account: Maps to 1200 - Accounts Receivable
5

Available Credit Calculation

Remaining credit available to this customer. Calculated as Credit Limit minus Current Balance. Prevents over-extension of credit.

Technical Details
  • Formula: creditLimit - currentBalance
  • Validation: Warns if new invoice exceeds available
  • Display: Negative values shown in red
  • Real-time: Updates as balances change
6

AR Aging Breakdown

Age analysis of outstanding receivables broken into Current (0-29 days), 30 days (30-59), 60 days (60-89), and 90+ days. Critical for collections management and bad debt provisioning.

Technical Details
  • Buckets: Current, 30, 60, 90+ days
  • Calculation: Based on invoice.dueDate vs today
  • Color Coding: Gray → Yellow → Orange → Red
  • Source: Aggregated from invoice aging data
7

Transaction History Tabs

Organized view of all customer interactions across Invoices, Payments, Statements, and Notes. Each tab provides focused context for managing customer relationships.

Technical Details
  • Tabs: Invoices, Payments, Statements, Notes
  • State Management: Tab switching via JS click handlers
  • Data Loading: Lazy load per tab for performance
  • Filters: Date range, status filters per tab
8

Invoice List for Customer

All invoices issued to this customer with status (Draft, Sent, Partial, Paid, Overdue), amounts, dates, and quick actions. Click to view/edit invoice details.

Technical Details
  • Query: invoices.where('customerId', '==', customer.id)
  • Sort: By invoiceDate descending (newest first)
  • Status Badges: Color-coded by invoice status
  • Actions: View PDF, Send Email, Record Payment, Edit
9

Payment History

Chronological record of all payments received from customer. Shows payment method, date, amount, and linked invoice. Essential for cash flow tracking and reconciliation.

Technical Details
  • Data Source: Payment records linked to customer
  • Methods: EFT, Credit Card, Cash, Cheque, Other
  • GL Integration: Creates DR Bank, CR AR entries
  • Reconciliation: Links to bank statement matches
10

Credit Hold Toggle

Manual override to block new transactions when balance exceeds limit or payment issues arise. Prevents new invoices/orders until resolved. Requires financial admin permission.

Technical Details
  • Field: customer.status = 'blocked'
  • Auto-trigger: When currentBalance > creditLimit
  • Effects: Blocks invoice creation, shows warnings
  • Permissions: financial_admin or higher to toggle
11

Quick Actions

Common workflows accessible without leaving customer view: Create Invoice (pre-fills customer), Record Payment (applies to customer balance), Send Statement (generates and emails AR statement).

Technical Details
  • New Invoice: Navigates to invoice creation with customerId
  • Record Payment: Opens payment dialog with customer context
  • Send Statement: Generates PDF via pdfService, emails
  • Integration: Uses DebtorService methods
12

Communication Log Section

Free-form notes and communication history with customer. Records calls, emails, issues, special terms, or any relevant relationship details for future reference.

Technical Details
  • Storage: customer.notes field (simple text)
  • Future: Planned expansion to timestamped note entries
  • Display: Chronological with user attribution
  • Privacy: Visible only to users with customer access
