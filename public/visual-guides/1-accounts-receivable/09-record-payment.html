Select how payment was received: EFT, Credit Card, Debit Order, Cash, or Cheque. Used for reconciliation and reporting.

Optional transaction reference, bank reference number, or cheque number. Helps with bank reconciliation and audit trail.

Notes Field

Optional notes about the payment (e.g., "Partial payment - customer paying balance next week").

Record Payment Button

Validates and records payment. Updates invoice status and amountPaid/amountDue. Can optionally post journal entry to GL (debit Bank, credit AR).
