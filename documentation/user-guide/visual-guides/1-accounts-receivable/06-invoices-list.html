Screen Guide

1

Create Invoice Button

Opens dialog to create a new invoice manually or from an accepted quote. Includes customer selection, line items, tax, and payment terms.

2

Summary Cards

Real-time KPIs showing total invoices amount, outstanding AR, overdue amounts, and payments received this month.

3

Search Bar

Search by invoice number, customer name, or amount. Real-time filtering.

4

Status Filter

Filter invoices by status: Draft, Sent, Partial, Paid, Overdue, or All.

5

Invoice Table Headers

Columns: Invoice #, Customer, Date, Due Date, Status, Amount Due, Actions.

6

Invoice Number (Clickable)

Click invoice number to view full invoice details dialog.

8

Status Badge

Color-coded: Blue=Sent, Green=Paid, Yellow=Partial, Red=Overdue, Gray=Draft.

9

Actions Dropdown

Actions: View, Edit, Send, Record Payment, Post to GL, Download PDF.

10

Amount Due Highlighting

Overdue amounts shown in red to draw attention.
