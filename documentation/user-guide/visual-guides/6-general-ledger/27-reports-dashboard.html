Screen Guide

Click on numbered badges or hover over UI elements to learn more about each feature.

1

Report Categories (Financial Statements, Management, AR/AP, Tax)

Reports organized into logical categories for easy navigation. Financial Statements for compliance, Management for operations, AR/AP for collections/payments.

Technical Details
  • Layout: Section-based organization with headers
  • Categories: financial_statements, ar_ap, management, tax_compliance
  • Purpose: User-friendly report discovery
2

Trial Balance Report Card

Lists all GL accounts with their debit and credit balances. Foundation for all other financial statements. Validates debits = credits.

Technical Details
  • Data Source: Aggregates from general ledger entries
  • Validation: Ensures total debits equal total credits
  • Parameters: As-of date, account range filter
3

Balance Sheet Report Card

Snapshot of financial position at a point in time. Shows Assets, Liabilities, and Equity. Key report for investors and lenders.

Technical Details
  • Formula: Assets = Liabilities + Equity
  • Account Types: asset, liability, equity accounts only
  • Comparison: Current vs prior period for trend analysis
4

Income Statement (P&L) Report Card

Performance over a period. Shows Revenue, Cost of Goods Sold, Expenses, and Net Income. Critical for profitability analysis.

Technical Details
  • Formula: Net Income = Revenue - COGS - Expenses
  • Account Types: revenue and expense accounts
  • Period-based: Month, Quarter, Year-to-Date comparisons
5

Cash Flow Statement Card

Tracks cash movements in Operating, Investing, and Financing activities. Shows how cash position changed over the period.

Technical Details
  • Method: Direct or Indirect cash flow calculation
  • Categories: Operating, Investing, Financing activities
  • Reconciliation: Beginning + Net Change = Ending Cash
6

AR Aging Report Card

Outstanding customer invoices grouped by age: Current, 1-30 days, 31-60 days, 61-90 days, 90+ days. Critical for collections management.

Technical Details
  • Data Source: Unpaid invoices from AR sub-ledger
  • Grouping: By customer and aging bucket
  • Calculation: Days outstanding = Today - Due Date
7

AP Aging Report Card

Outstanding vendor bills grouped by age. Helps prioritize payments and manage vendor relationships.

Technical Details
  • Data Source: Unpaid vendor bills from AP sub-ledger
  • Grouping: By supplier and aging bucket
  • Use Case: Cash planning, vendor payment prioritization
8

General Ledger Detail Report Card

Transaction-level detail for all GL accounts with running balances. Used for account reconciliation and audit trail.

Technical Details
  • Data Source: All ledger entries from journal entries
  • Display: Date, reference, description, debit, credit, balance
  • Filters: Account, date range, source type
9

Export Format Options (PDF, Excel, CSV)

All reports support multiple export formats. PDF for distribution, Excel for analysis, CSV for data import into other systems.

Technical Details
  • PDF: Using pdfMake for formatted reports with company branding
  • Excel: XLSX generation with formulas and formatting
  • CSV: Raw data export for external tools
